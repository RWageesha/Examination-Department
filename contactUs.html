Frequently Asked Questions

How can I request my academic transcript?

To request an academic transcript, you need to submit a completed Transcript Request Form (available in the Student Downloads section) along with the payment receipt. Processing typically takes 5-7 working days.

When will examination results be published?

Examination results are usually published within 4-6 weeks after the completion of examinations. Results are first approved by the Board of Examiners and the Senate before being released to students through faculty notice boards and the student portal.

How do I apply for special consideration for missed examinations?

Special consideration applications must be submitted within 7 days of the examination using the Special Consideration Form. Supporting documentation (such as medical certificates) must be attached. Each case is evaluated individually by the Special Consideration Committee.

How can I obtain a detailed degree certificate?

Detailed degree certificates can be requested by submitting the Detailed Degree Certificate Request Form with the appropriate fee payment. Processing time is approximately 10 working days. Graduates must have cleared all outstanding dues to the university.
